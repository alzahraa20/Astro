# Astro

Astro simplifies NASA data of earth and space sciences for kids by converting normal explanations to friendly conversations with videos and games.
in addition to motivating kids to enjoy learning with interactive activities. 

## features:
### astro has two categories earth and space, each category has different topics containing
* conversations between Astro (our lovely kid) and his teacher
* animation videos 
* quizzes in form of games

Note: we apply that on two topics, the atmosphere and solar system as a prototype for our idea

### astro also has interactive activities
* age calculator on all planets in years and days
* weight calculator on other planets and our moon
* earth satellite map
* interactive videos for building crafts (ex: rocket)
